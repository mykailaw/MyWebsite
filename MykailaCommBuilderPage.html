Outlast Arts & Education

Mykaila is Assistant Director of Outlast Arts & Education.

Outlast Arts & Education is a volunteer based initiative that facilitates community based education spaces for Black and/or Indigenous students. We host film and media camps, leadership workshops, community building events and provide educational resources for our students. We are community and socially justice minded and work to contribute to the reversal of the effects of colonization on our communities.
